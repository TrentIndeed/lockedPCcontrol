E-LEARNING / TRAINING COURSE RULES:

NEVER GO BACKWARD:
  - NEVER click sidebar/menu items for lessons or sections you have ALREADY
    completed or that appear BEFORE your current position.
  - If a sidebar shows lessons 1-5 and you are on lesson 4, NEVER click
    lessons 1, 2, or 3. Only click lesson 5 or later if needed.
  - If you accidentally go backward, immediately click NEXT or use the
    sidebar to return to where you were.
  - The sidebar/menu is for reference — your PRIMARY navigation is the
    NEXT button. Only use the sidebar to jump FORWARD, never backward.

NUMBERED CIRCLES / HOTSPOTS (CRITICAL — this is the #1 blocker):
  Training slides frequently have numbered circles, hotspots, or tiles
  labeled 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 etc. These are MANDATORY interactive elements.
  - You MUST click EVERY numbered element, starting from 1.
  - After clicking one: a popup, inset, or detail panel will appear.
    Read it, then dismiss it (click "X", "Close", "Return to main slide",
    or click the next number directly).
  - Then click the next number: 2, then 3, then 4, etc.
  - NEXT will NOT activate until you have clicked ALL numbers.
  - If you click one and nothing seems to happen, look for content that
    appeared in a panel or overlay — you may need to scroll within it
    or close it before the next number becomes clickable.
  - Do NOT skip numbers. Do NOT try NEXT before finishing all of them.
  - AFTER completing ALL numbers, IMMEDIATELY click NEXT to advance.
    Do NOT re-click numbers you already completed.

WHEN NEXT BUTTON DOES NOT WORK — diagnose in this order:
  1. NUMBERED ELEMENTS: Are there numbered circles/hotspots on the slide
     you haven't clicked? Click ALL of them in order. This is the answer
     90% of the time.
  2. WAIT: A video or animation may need to finish first.
     Use {"type":"wait","duration":15} and try NEXT again after.
     If you already waited once and it didn't help, do NOT wait again.
  3. INTERACT: Look for other clickable items ON the slide itself
     — tiles, tabs, expandable sections, checkboxes, or "Submit" buttons.
  4. SCROLL DOWN: Check for content or controls below the visible area.
  5. ANSWER A QUESTION: An unanswered quiz or knowledge check may be
     blocking progression.
  Do NOT click sidebar items when NEXT doesn't work. The problem is almost
  always on the CURRENT page, not in the navigation menu.

QUIZZES AND QUESTIONS:
  - Read the FULL question and ALL answer choices before selecting.
  - Think about what the training material covered — match key terms.
  - If wrong and you can retry, the feedback often hints at the answer.
  - Watch for traps: "all of the above," absolutes like "never"/"always."
